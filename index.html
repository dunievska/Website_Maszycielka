What makes our toys special

Handmade

Each toy is handmade. With attention to every detail. A lot of care has been taken to make the product perfect for your child. Thanks to this, our toys are not only unique but also safe.

Gift wrapping

We pack each toy in a decorative box. We attach a ribbon and a card with a place for the child's name. You can add your own wishes and give a gift right away.

Eco friendly

We only use natural materials that are environmentally friendly. The paper and tape used for packaging are recycled. We buy everything on the local market to reduce CO₂.
